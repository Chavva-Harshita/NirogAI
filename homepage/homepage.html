Comprehensive Healthcare Features

NirogAI brings together advanced technology and healthcare expertise to serve communities across India

Multilingual AI Chatbot

Answers health-related queries in multiple Indian languages + English. Provides preventive care tips, vaccination info, and outbreak updates.

Symptom Checker

Users enter symptoms in plain language. Chatbot suggests possible causes + whether to consult a doctor. Clear disclaimers to avoid self-diagnosis risks.

Vaccination & Health Reminders

Push notifications or SMS reminders for vaccines, check-ups, or wellness tips. Customizable reminders for family members.

Outbreak Alerts

Real-time updates about diseases or public health advisories in the user's region. Data sourced from government & WHO APIs.

Multilingual & Inclusive UI

Simple dashboard with big icons, voice/text input. Supports low literacy & semi-urban users.

Nearby Health Centers Locator

Integrates Google Maps API to show nearby government hospitals, PHCs, vaccination camps.

Preventive Health Knowledge Hub

Short, culturally appropriate articles/videos. Covers hygiene, nutrition, maternal care, mental health, etc.

Human Escalation Option

If chatbot cannot answer, users can connect with a helpline/health worker.

Analytics & Feedback Loop

Tracks chatbot accuracy & usage trends. Collects user feedback to improve responses.

Privacy & Security

Anonymous usage, no sensitive data stored. End-to-end encrypted conversations.
